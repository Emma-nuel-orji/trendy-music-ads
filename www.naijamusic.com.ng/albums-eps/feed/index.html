Promising Nigerian singer Morravey comes through with her debut EP titled “Ravi”. 

Morravey, one of the two new artistes signed to DMW, has just released her debut project the “Ravi” EP. The wait is finally over and Davido showcases his new female artiste Morravey with her latest body of work. The Nigerian music space got to know her after she was featured on Davido’s highly acclaimed third studio album “Timeless“.

The singer was featured on the track “In The Garden” and she owned the song to the admiration of listeners. Since her entry into the limelight, new fans have been requesting new music from her. Now you have a new handful of songs from Morravey.

The “Ravi” EP has 5 tracks “What Is Love“, “High Again“, “Condo“, “My Baby” and “Magician” which features Davido. It is with much excitement that Morravey and DMW present this EP, enjoy!
